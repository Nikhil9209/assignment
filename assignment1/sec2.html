SIR RATAN TATA

Early life and education

Ratan Tata was born on in Bombay (now Mumbai),. He is son of Naval tata . He was born on 28th decmeber 1937.

Tata studied at the The campion school until 8th grade . He cotinued his study Cathedral and John Connon School in Mumbai, the Bishop Cotton School in Shimla, and the Riverdale Country School in New York City, from which he graduated in 1955.

After high school, Tata enrolled in Cornell University, from which he graduated with a bachelor's degree in architecture in 1962.

In 2008 Tata gifted Cornell $50million, becoming th lagest donor in the university's history .

Completed the Advanced Management Program from Harvard Business School in 1975.
